Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Welcome to SuperCart. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your email address and/or phone number for authentication and account management.

How We Use Your Data

  • Personalization: To improve your shopping experience and offer personalized recommendations.
  • Order Tracking: To generate bills, track your purchases, and verify your exit from the store.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with third-party services (like payment gateways) necessary to complete your transactions or as required by law.
